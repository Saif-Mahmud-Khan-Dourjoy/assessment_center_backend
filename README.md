## Assessment Center (AC)

An Online Assessment Centre will add innovation in traditional learning and it will act as one-stop-destination for online examination preparation. It will be a fully automated platform where contributor (question solution developer) will put their effort and assessee will find and learn how to assess themselves as of their field of interest.

-	Assessment Center for all sorts of education, training, courses, general knowledge etc
-	Individual can come and find their required test fields to realize their levels
-	Contributors can put and set questions and answers to throw to the assesses
-	Contributors may set the price for their solutions for those who buys the assessment
